About Me

Hello, My name is Matthew Bellario. I am a student at the Illinois Institute of Technology, going for a major in Information Technology and Management. Growing up, I was with brand new technology constantly expanding and growing around me. When I was young we owned an Ipod Touch and Ipod Nano. In middle school, our class was the first generation to use Ipads for learning in school. In high school, we had chromebooks to use for class all our four years in high school so I was always surrounded by technology. Because of these involvement with technology I became interested and sought to learn more about technology.
